About Crumb&Co. :

Welcome to Crumb & Co. Bakery — where every creation tells a story and every bite brings comfort. Founded with a deep love for artisan baking and a commitment to quality, Crumb & Co. is more than just a bakery — it’s a place where tradition meets creativity, and where our community gathers to enjoy life’s simple pleasures.

At Crumb & Co., we pride ourselves on using only the finest, locally sourced ingredients to craft our wide range of baked goods. From crusty sourdough loaves and buttery croissants to rich chocolate cakes and custom-designed desserts, everything we make is fresh, flavorful, and made from scratch daily.

We also cater to various dietary needs with gluten-free, vegan, and low-sugar options — because we believe everyone deserves a taste of something wonderful.

At Crumb & Co., community is at the heart of everything we do. We’re proud to be a neighborhood favorite, a gathering place for friends and families, and a part of your celebrations and everyday moments.

Come for the crumbs — stay for the company.
